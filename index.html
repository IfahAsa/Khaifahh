Proyek

Nct Dream

A vibrant project celebrating the dynamic energy and creativity of the NCT Dream group. This project showcases various fan interactions, concept art, and highlights from their music videos, aimed at bringing fans closer to the group’s artistic journey..

WayV

An exploration of WayV, the Chinese sub-unit of NCT. This project features music releases, dance practices, and behind-the-scenes content, emphasizing their unique style and cultural influence. It offers a glimpse into their performances and the stories behind their songs..

Nct 127

A comprehensive overview of NCT 127, focusing on their chart-topping hits and groundbreaking performances. The project includes an analysis of their musical evolution, thematic elements in their albums, and the impact they have on the global music scene..
